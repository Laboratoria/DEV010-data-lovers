Game of Thrones survivors:

"Game of Thrones" is a fantasy novel written by American author George R. R. Martin in 1996. It is the first installment in the series "Song of ice and fire". The novel is characterized by its medieval aesthetics, the description of numerous well-detailed characters, the contrasting points of view of the multiple protagonists, its plot with unexpected twists and a subtle and moderate use of magical aspects so common in other works.
